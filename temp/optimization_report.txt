- The search used a two-stage grid: Stage 1 tested 144 coarse

  the Stage 1 winner.  Interactions between the discrete

  parameters (N_ACOMP, N_AROMA) are only partially captured.
- Mean_QC is the simple arithmetic mean of Validity, Quality,
  and Sensitivity.  All three are on [0, 1].  If one score
  matters more for your analysis, consider weighting or
  selecting by that score alone.
- The DataSensitivity score depends on the number of valid
  subjects remaining after outlier exclusion; aggressive
  denoising may raise Validity/Quality but lower Sensitivity
  by reducing effective DOF.
